# Chapter 11 – The Great Offensive and Final Victory

## 1. Significance of the Battle of Sakarya
- **Answer:** End of the Turkish retreat that began in 1683  
- **Memory Trick:** Sakarya = no more retreat  
- **Arabic:** نهاية التراجع التركي المستمر منذ عام 1683

## 2. Country that signed the Treaty of Kars
- **Answer:** Azerbaijan  
- **Arabic:** أذربيجان

## 3. Preparatory measure before the Great Offensive
- **Answer:** Increasing the army to 200,000 soldiers  
- **Arabic:** زيادة عدد الجيش إلى 200 ألف جندي

## 4. Greek defensive preparation
- **Answer:** Fortifying the Afyon region  
- **Arabic:** تحصين منطقة أفيون

## 5. Starting point of the Great Offensive
- **Answer:** South of Afyon  
- **Arabic:** جنوب أفيون

## 6. Most important strategy of the Turkish army
- **Answer:** Surprise attack from the south of Afyon  
- **Arabic:** هجوم مباغت من جنوب أفيون

## 7. Location NOT captured on August 27, 1922
- **Answer:** Dumlupınar  
- **Arabic:** دوملوبينار

## 8. Reason Greek army moved northwest
- **Answer:** Insufficient transportation infrastructure near İzmir  
- **Arabic:** ضعف البنية التحتية للنقل قرب إزمير

## 9. Mustafa Kemal’s description after the Offensive
- **Answer:** Clear and heavy defeat of the enemy  
- **Arabic:** هزيمة واضحة وثقيلة للعدو

## 10. General outcome of the Great Offensive
- **Answer:** Complete end of Greek occupation  
- **Arabic:** انتهاء الاحتلال اليوناني بالكامل
