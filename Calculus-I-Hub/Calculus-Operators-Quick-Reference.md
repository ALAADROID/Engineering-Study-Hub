# Calculus Operators Quick Reference

| Operation          | Symbol / Notation                           | Meaning               |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Integration        | $\int f(x)\, dx$                            | Area under curve      |
| Differentiation    | $\frac{d}{dx} f(x)$ or $f'(x)$              | Rate of change        |
| Higher derivative  | $\frac{d^2}{dx^2} f(x)$ or $f''(x)$         | Change of slope       |
| Operator form      | $\left(\frac{d}{dx}\right)(f(x))$           | Apply derivative      |
| Partial derivative | $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$             | Change in one variable|

> 𝑓(𝑥)𝑑𝑥 ≠ 𝑓′(𝑥)
