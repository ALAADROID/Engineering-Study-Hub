## ⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT RULES:

**Rule 1:**
* **if n>30** → use Z (normal)

<br>

**Rule 2:**
* **p given** → proportion formula
* **$\overline{X}$ given** → mean formula

<br>

**Rule 3:**
* **CI** → always ±
* **Hypothesis** → compare Z

<br>

## How to choose formula:  
| Case                 | Use                |
| -------------------- | ------------------ |
| Given p (percentage) | proportion formula |
| Given mean (X̄)       | mean formula       |
| Hypothesis (H0, H1)  | z-test             |
| n > 30               | use Z (normal)     |

<br>
