## Basic symbols:

- $n$ → sample size:  
> number of data (students, items . . .).

- $\overline{X}$ (X-bar) → sample mean:
> average of sample.

- $u$ → population mean:  
> real (unkonwn) average.

- $p$ → sample proportion:
> percentage (like 0.34, 0.67).

- $1-p$ or $q$ → remaining %

- $Z$ → number from table
> **Common values:**  
> 90% - 1.64  
> 95% - 1.96  
> 99% - 2.33

<br>

### Spread / error:

- $s$ → sample standard deviation  
> how data is spread

- $SE$ → standard error:  
> for mean: $SE=s/√n$  
> for proportion: $SE=√\overline{p(1-p)}/n$
