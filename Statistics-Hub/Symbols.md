## Basic symbols:

- $n$ ‚Üí sample size:  
> number of data (students, items . . .).

- $\overline{X}$ (X-bar) ‚Üí sample mean:
> average of sample.

- $u$ ‚Üí population mean:  
> real (unkonwn) average.

- $p$ ‚Üí sample proportion:
> percentage (like 0.34, 0.67).

- $Œ±$ ‚Üí significance level:  
> $Œ±$=1‚àíconfidence level  
> It is the error we allow (risk of being wrong)

- $Z$ ‚Üí number from table
> **Common values:**  
> 90% - 1.64  
> 95% - 1.96  
> 99% - 2.33

- $1-p$ or $q$ ‚Üí remaining %

### Spread / error:

- $s$ ‚Üí sample standard deviation  
> how data is spread

- $SE$ ‚Üí standard error:  
> for mean: $SE=s/‚àön$  
> for proportion: $SE$=‚àö ( $p$ (1- $p$ )/ $n$ )
