## 🧠 Full Solution Strategy  
### ✅ Steps to Solve:
  - Check if equation is homogeneous
      - Look for y/x
  - Substitute
      - u=y/x , y=ux
  - Differentiate
      - dy/dx = u+x(du/dx)
  - Plug into original equation
  - Simplify → Separable form
  - Integrate both sides
  - Back-substitute
      - u=y/x

## 🔥 Key Takeaways:
  - Homogeneous equations depend on y/x
  - Substitution always simplifies the equation
  - Final goal → make it separable
  - Don’t forget to substitute back at the end
