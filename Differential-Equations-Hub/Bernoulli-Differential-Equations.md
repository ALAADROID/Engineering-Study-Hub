## What is Bernoulli Equation?  
- A differential equation of the form:  
  dx/dy​+P(x)y=Q(x)y<sup>n</sup>  
- It is nonlinear (because of y<sup>n</sup>)  
- But we can transform it into linear  
> [!IMPORTANT]
> If n=0 → becomes linear directly.  
> If n=1 → also linear.

##  Main Trick (Substitution)  
- We use substitution:  
  v=y<sup>1−n</sup>  
- Then differentiate:  
  dx/dv​=(1−n)y<sup>−n</sup>dx/dy​
- After substitution, the equation becomes:  
  dx/dv​+(1−n)P(x)v=(1−n)Q(x)
- Now it is:  
  Linear differential equation in v
