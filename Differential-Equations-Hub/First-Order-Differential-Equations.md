### Step 2 - Find Integrating Factor:

### Step 3 - Multiply whole equation:
  - Multiply EVERYTHING by u(x)  
  > d/dx [u(x)y]

### Step 4 - Integrate:  
  - d/dx​ [μy] = μQ(x)

  - uy = ∫u(x)Q(x)dx+c
